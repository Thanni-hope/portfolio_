About me

I am Ginne Sai Thanooj Reddy, a third-year B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering student at the National Institute of Technology, Calicut, with a strong academic foundation in Data Structures, Algorithms, Database Management Systems, and Operating Systems. Proficient in C/C++, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and MySQL, I possess hands-on experience with development tools such as Git, GitHub, and VSCode. I am currently expanding my expertise in full-stack development through a comprehensive course on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB, enabling me to design and implement projects that integrate robust back-end functionality with intuitive front-end interfaces, emphasizing efficiency, scalability, and user experience.

In addition to my technical pursuits, I have actively contributed to large-scale event management at NIT Calicut, including Tathva’24 and Ragam’25, overseeing event planning, sponsor coordination, logistics, and creative content development for the Forum for Dance and Dramatics (DnD) club. These experiences have strengthened my leadership, organizational, and communication skills, along with my ability to perform under high-pressure environments. I am committed to delivering high-quality, impactful solutions by blending technical proficiency with creativity, while continuously adapting to emerging technologies and industry best practices.

Education

Completed my schooling at Kakatiya High School with a perfect 10 CGPA, followed by intermediate studies at Narayana Junior College, where I scored 984/1000. I am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Calicut, with a focus on mastering core computer science concepts and modern development technologies.

Experiences

Actively involved in event management, coordination, and content creation at NIT Calicut. I have contributed to flagship events such as Tathva’24 and Ragam’25, where I managed technical event planning, coordinated with sponsors, designed problem statements, and oversaw logistics. As a member of the Forum for Dance and Dramatics (DnD) content team, I have also written engaging scripts and event narratives that enhanced audience participation.

Projects

Developed multiple projects combining front-end design and back-end functionality, including a URL Shortener, a Dijkstra’s Algorithm Visualizer, and a Social Feed Web App. These projects showcase my ability to build efficient, user-friendly, and scalable solutions using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB. My work emphasizes clean code, intuitive design, and optimized performance.
